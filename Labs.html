COMPUTERs LAB

A diploma computer department includes several specialized labs such as Python, C, C++, Java, RDBMS, Data Structures, and Web Designing. These labs help students practice programming, build logic, work with databases, understand algorithms, and create websites. Each lab provides hands-on experience with real coding environments, software tools, and project development, allowing students to gain strong technical and problem-solving skills essential for IT and software careers.

PHYSICS LAB

A diploma physics lab provides hands-on experiments using instruments like lenses, prisms, and electrical setups to help students understand basic principles of motion, light, electricity, and measurement used in engineering.

CHEMISTRY LAB

A diploma chemistry lab provides hands-on experiments with chemicals, reactions, and material testing using essential lab equipment, helping students understand chemical properties and safe lab practices.

BEEE LAB

A diploma basic electrical and electronics lab helps students learn fundamental concepts by working with circuits, components, and measuring instruments to understand how electrical and electronic systems function.

DE LAB

A diploma digital electronics lab lets students work with logic gates, ICs, and circuits to design and test digital systems like counters and adders, helping them understand how modern electronic devices operate.

HARDWARE LAB

A diploma hardware and networking lab provides hands-on training in assembling, troubleshooting, and maintaining computer systems and networks. Students learn to work with internal components, install operating systems, configure routers and switches, set up LAN connections, and diagnose network issues. The lab helps them gain practical skills needed for IT support, system maintenance, and basic network administration.
