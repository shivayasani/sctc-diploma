Diploma Courses

Explore career-focused diploma programs with hands-on training and excellent placement opportunities.
  • Diploma in Computer Science (CSE)
    Duration: 3 Years  |  Eligibility: 10th Pass
    A 3-year technical diploma focused on computer programming, software development, networking, databases, and modern technologies. It prepares students for IT and software-related careers.

    📘 What You Learn
    Programming languages (C, C++, Java, Python)
    Data Structures
    Web development (HTML, CSS, JS, PHP)
    Database Management (SQL, RDBMS)
    Computer Networks
    Operating Systems
    Software Engineering
    Cyber Security basics
    Cloud & AI basics (in updated syllabus)
  • Diploma in Electronics & Communication (ECE)
    Duration: 3 Years  |  Eligibility: 10th Pass
    A 3-year diploma that focuses on electronics, communication systems, circuits, and modern digital technology. It teaches how electronic devices work and how communication systems (mobile, TV, internet) are designed.

    📘 What You Learn
    Electronic devices & circuits
    Digital electronics
    Communication systems (analog & digital)
    Microprocessors & microcontrollers
    Embedded systems basics
    Networking & signal processing
    Consumer electronics
    PCB design basics
  • Diploma in Mechanical Engineering (ME)
    Duration: 3 Years  |  Eligibility: 10th Pass
    A 3-year technical diploma that focuses on machines, manufacturing, designing, and maintenance. It deals with how machines work and how industries produce goods.

    📘 What You Learn
    Engineering drawing & drafting
    Material science
    Thermodynamics
    Fluid mechanics
    Strength of materials
    Manufacturing technology (lathe, milling, welding, CNC)
    Machine design
    Automobile engineering basics
    CAD/CAM software
  • Diploma in Civil Engineering (CE)
    Duration: 3 Years  |  Eligibility: 10th Pass
    A 3-year course focused on the construction, design, and maintenance of buildings, roads, bridges, and other infrastructure.

    📘What You Learn
    Building construction
    Surveying
    Engineering drawing
    Concrete & structural basics
    Estimation & costing
    CAD software (AutoCAD)
  • Diploma in Electrical And Electronics Engineering (EEE)
    Duration: 3 Years  |  Eligibility: 10th Pass
    A 3-year technical course that teaches the fundamentals of electricity, electronics, power systems, and electrical machines. Students learn both theory and practical skills needed for electrical and electronics industries.

    📘What You Learn
    Electrical circuits & wiring
    Electronic devices & circuits
    Electrical machines (motors, transformers)
    Power systems & power electronics
    Digital electronics & control systems
