Who are we?

Sri Chaitanya Technical Campus was established in the year 2008 at Sheriguda, Ibrahimpatnam, R.R. Dist. It is affiliated to the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTUH), Hyderabad, and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Government of India. The institution is sponsored by Sri Venkata Sai Educational Society.

The campus spreads across 5.0 acres of serene surroundings, located just 10 km from the city. With excellent connectivity, open spaces, and green surroundings, it provides the perfect atmosphere for Engineering & Management education.

Mission

To provide quality education in major engineering disciplines at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels, ensuring a balanced blend of academic, professional, and extracurricular activities. Our mission is to nature innovation and ethical values while preparing students to meet the changing demands of industry and society.

Vision

To develop Sri Chaitanya into a globally recognized institution of higher learning known for excellence in engineering and technology, producing innovative, versatile, and globally competitive engineers, and fostering a culture of collaboration and lifelong learning.
